Marvin Jones added to injury report with ankle ailment

Fri, Oct 5th 6:44pm EDT
By Blaine Blontz

Marvin Jones was a limited participant in Friday's practice and is questionable entering the team's Week 5 contest against the Packers. (Source: Mike Rothstein on Twitter)

Fantasy Impact: Jones was a late add to the injury report with the ankle ailment. As such, he's questionable to play Week 5 in a solid matchup against the Packers. Should he sit, Kenny Golladay and Golden Tate would likely take on more targets, though Golladay could be the larger beneficiary considering he's also lining up on the outside.
